---- SPECIAL MECHANICS ----
-- Kobold nations will start with a special egg incubation site, the Hatchery.
-- Building up their forts to maximum level will also create a Hatchery through
-- event modding.
-- Rituals (and perhaps random events?) will allow spawning special, immovable
-- Dragon Egg units in provinces that contain a Hatchery.
-- These Dragon Eggs will naturally accrue XP over time. They have an #xpshape
-- that will eventually trigger, making them hatch into Dragon fledglings.
-- Fledglings are Units that can be led around. They will also have an #xpshape
-- and eventually become bigger and bigger Dragons.
-- Adult Dragons are Commanders, but they are #homesick. They can only stay away
-- from their home fort for so long before dying.
-- If there are a male and a female Adult Dragon in a fort, they can also lay
-- Eggs every Spring. Single Dragons, however (when there is only one in a fort)
-- will gain extra XP through regular events, and accumulate wealth (extra upkeep?)
-- until a certain amount is reached, at which point a Dragon Hoard site will be
-- created. As the Dragon's Greed and Jealousy knows no bounds, any Egg in the
-- same province as a Dragon Hoard will be killed (future competitors).
-- Obtaining a Dragon Hoard fundamentally changes the nature of the Dragon. They will
-- become Hoard Dragons, reaching new levels of power (gaining magic paths?) that
-- lesser Dragon only interested in mating cannot hope to achieve.
