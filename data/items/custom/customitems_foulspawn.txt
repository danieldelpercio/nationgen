--- #new initializes both armors and weapons
--- #armor will indicate that the item is armor
--- Use exact syntax you'd use for dom6 except for #newweapon, #newarmor and #end
--- Dominions mod commands are prefixed by #command; i.e. #command "#hp 5"

#new customarmor
